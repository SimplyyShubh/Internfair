INTERNFAIR

Providing opportunities to be hired as interns in leading startup and firms.

What is it ?

In order to ignite entrepreneurial spirit in the students, E-Cell proudly presents INTERNFAIR in collaboration with the Training & Placement Cell at MUJ

It is aimed at giving the students a taste of the professional world and provide the companies hard-working and talented additions to their workforce. The effort of this events is to provide students with a closer insight into the entrepreneurial world and bring to them some real-world business challenges
